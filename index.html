What is the BDSM Test?

The BDSM Test is a fork of Sapply Values that aims to put sexuality on a compass. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a compass with the values of dominant or submissive and experimentalist or vanilla, along with a meter from asxeul to hypersexual. There are questions in the test.

Privacy notice

Absolutely none of your answers will be recorded or stored in any way, shape, or form.
